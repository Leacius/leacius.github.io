About Me

Mr. Chang is currently pursuing his Ph.D. at the Institute of Information Systems and Applications at National Tsing Hua University. He is also a member of the Multimedia Information System Laboratory (MISLab), under the guidance of Professor Min-Chun Hu. His research centers on computer vision, specifically in the area of action recognition. He is developing AI systems aimed at analyzing human actions in broadcast videos, particularly for table tennis players.
